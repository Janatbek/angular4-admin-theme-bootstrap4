Widget administration
Click on the name labels of any of the widgets in the master list below to register or unregister the widget in your custom settings. Unregistered widgets will appear grey and will not show up in any views you navigate to within the platform. Registered widgets will be blue. To move or order widgets for a particular view, use the windows button at the top right of the screen when you are in the context-based view you want to customize to group and lay out the widgets.
